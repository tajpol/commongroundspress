About

Common Grounds Press is a nonprofit dedicated to telling the stories and history of diverse people and communities through archival practice, photography, publishing, and educational opportunities. We focus on a broad range of cultural expressions including the countercultural, the controversial, and the obscene, while exploring their connections to heritage architecture, public spaces, & art history. Our work fosters a pluralistic understanding of history and culture, grounded in place-based research and visual documentation. Click here to visit our links & follow our social media and more!
